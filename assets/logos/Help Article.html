How do I upgrade from a Premier to a Business account?

Here's how:
  1. Log in to your PayPal account.
  2. Click Profile near the top of the page.
  3. Click My personal info.
  4. Click Update near Business Information.
  5. Click Upgrade.
  6. Select a business type and click Continue.
  7. Enter your business information and click Continue.
